# RS-05: Genome-to-Tension Bridge

**Status:** Open  
**Priority:** High  
**Integration Module**

## Goal

Создать мост между 3D-топологией хроматина и рисками меротелии в митозе.

## Background

Топология хроматина влияет на риск меротелии кинетохоров в митозе. Необходимо связать ARCHCODE Core с Cellular Kernel через tension mapping.

## Method

1. **Topology Analysis**
   - Extract TAD boundaries from archcode_core
   - Calculate boundary strengths
   - Map boundaries to chromosome positions

2. **Tension Mapping**
   - Map boundary strength to kinetochore tension risk
   - Calculate merotelic attachment probability
   - Generate risk scores per chromosome

3. **Cellular Kernel Integration**
   - Feed tension risks to cellular_kernel
   - Adjust misattach_probability based on topology
   - Integrate with LTL verifier

## Deliverables

- `genome_to_tension/topology_analyzer.py` - Topology analysis
- `genome_to_tension/tension_mapper.py` - Tension risk mapping
- `genome_to_tension/kernel_bridge.py` - Cellular Kernel integration
- `config/genome_to_tension.yaml` - Mapping parameters
