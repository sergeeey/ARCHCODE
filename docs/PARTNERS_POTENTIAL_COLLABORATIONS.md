Purpose: List of potential partner types and short email template for outreach.

## 1. Partner Profiles (non-exhaustive)

| Type | Who | Why |
|------|-----|-----|
| **Beta-thalassemia / hemoglobinopathy labs** | Groups with HBB genetics, patient cohorts, erythroid models | Direct relevance to HBB promoter/LCR biology; may have cell lines and interest in VUS/pearl reclassification. |

We are seeking a lab with erythroid cell models (e.g. K562, HUDEP-2) and capacity for RT-PCR and/or Capture Hi-C to test whether these variants show the predicted functional impact (splice/transcript change, promoter–LCR contact change).

We provide a formal validation protocol, a priority variant shortlist (3–5 variants), and all ARCHCODE predictions. Success criteria are explicit; we will report negative or inconclusive results as such. No clinical reclassification is proposed without validation data.
